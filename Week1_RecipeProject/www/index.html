Week 1 - Recipe Project

My Favorite Applesauce Recipe

List of Ingredients

  • Lemon Juice (1 to 2 TBSP)
  • Medium Applies (3 to 4) cored, cut into larges pieces

Follow these steps using a medium to highspeed blender:

  1. Place all ingredients into blender in the order listed and secure lid
  2. Start the blender
  3. Blend until desired consistency is reached
  4. Stop the blender

Preparation Time: 10 minutes
